Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Is root canal treatment painful?

At Healthy-Dent, root canal treatments are performed with modern anaesthesia and advanced techniques. Most patients report feeling little to no discomfort during the procedure. Our goal is always a painless, stress-free experience.

Do you treat young children?

Absolutely. We have a dedicated paediatric approach and our doctors are specially trained to make children feel safe and comfortable. Many kids actually look forward to their visits — as evidenced by our patients' reviews!

How long does a typical appointment take?

Simple procedures like fillings and cleanings take 30–45 minutes. Complex treatments like implants or aligners may require multiple visits. We always explain the complete timeline before starting any treatment.

Do I need an appointment or can I walk in?

We welcome both walk-ins and appointments. Sunday visits are by appointment only. To avoid waiting, we recommend booking via WhatsApp in advance — it's quick and easy.

Where exactly is the clinic located?

We are located in SD Complex, Near Technopark, Mukkolakkal Signal, Attinkuzhy Road, Kazhakkoottam — easily accessible from Technopark and the NH Bypass. Use our Google Maps link for turn-by-turn directions.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), and card payments for your convenience. We keep billing simple and completely transparent.
